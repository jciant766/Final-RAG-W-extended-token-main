## SUBSIDIARY LEGISLATION 246.04

## IMMOVABLE PROPERTY (ACQUISITION BY NONRESIDENTS) (VALUE OF IMMOVABLES) ORDER

1st January, 1999

LEGAL NOTICE 98 of 1999, as amended by Legal Notice 411 of 2007.

1. The title of this Order is Immovable Property (Acquisition by Non-Residents) (Value of Immovables) Order.
2. For the purpose of paragraph ( b )  of  the  proviso  to  article 5(1) of the Immovable Property (Acquisition by Non-Residents) Act, the value of immovable property referred to in the said paragraph and in relation to which the Minister shall not withhold his permission is being established at €70,000 in the case of an apartment and €117,000 in the case of any other property.

For the purposes  of this  Order  "apartment"  means  a one-storey residence, howsoever called, in a vertical block of at least two residential units overlying each other and built on a common site.

Title.

Establishment of value. Amended by: L.N. 411 of 2007. Cap. 246.
