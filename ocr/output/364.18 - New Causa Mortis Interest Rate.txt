## SUBSIDIARY LEGISLATION 364.18

## RATE OF INTEREST IN THE DUTY ON DOCUMENTS AND TRANSFERS ACT REGULATIONS

1st January, 2020

LEGAL NOTICE 167 of 2020.

1. The title of these regulations is the Rate of Interest in the Duty on Documents and Transfers Act Regulations.
2. In these regulations, unless the context otherwise requires-

"the Act" means the Duty on Documents and Transfers Act.

3. Save as otherwise expressly provided, interest under the Act shall apply at the rate of zero point three three per centum (0.33%) for every thirty (30 days) or part thereof.
4. The interest applicable in paragraph (ii) of sub-article (4) of article 35 of the  Act shall apply at the rate of four per centum (4%) per annum.

Title.

Interpretation.

Cap. 364.

Rate of Interest.
