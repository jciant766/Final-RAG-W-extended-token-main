## SUBSIDIARY LEGISLATION 16.14

## ELECTRONIC REGISTER (TERMINATION OF MANDATES) REGULATIONS

2nd December, 2016 *

LEGAL NOTICE 404 of 2016.

1. The  title  of  these  regulations  is  the  Electronic  Register (Termination of Mandates) Regulations.
2. The Chief Notary to Government shall maintain an electronic database which shall contain an electronic register with regards to applications submitted for the termination of special mandates on immovable property or general mandates between physical persons.
3. (1) The  termination  of  special  mandates  on  immovable property between physical persons and the termination of general mandates given between physical persons shall be notified by the mandatory or by any person having an interest in the mandate to the Chief Notary to Government.
- (2) The  notification  shall  be  made  by  submitting  one  of  the application forms found in the Schedule:

Provided  that  all  application  forms  submitted,  including application forms submitted electronically, shall be processed by the Chief Notary to Government on working days until three in the afternoon (3.00 p.m.) and the electronic register shall be updated electronically every working day.

- (3) A  fee  of  fifty  euro  (€50)  shall  be  charged  by  the  Chief Notary to Government for every application submitted.
- (4) The termination notified to the Chief Notary to Government shall be entered in the electronic register on the same day when the notification is made.
- (5) Notaries  Public  and  banks  shall  be  deemed  to  be  notified with the termination of the mandates referred to in sub-article (1) upon the entry of the termination in the online register.
- (6) No action for damages shall lie against the Chief Notary to Government for failure to comply with any of the provisions of these regulations except on the basis of wilful acts done in bad faith or of gross negligence.
